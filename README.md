# Objective 💡 
This Project is aimed to perform analysis on the sales of products of a Company with respect to some features like Region, Categories, Cities and Channel. 

# Dataset 📃 
- Date
- Region
- City
- Customer Type
- Channel
- Product
- Category
- Unit Price
- Quantity
- Sales Rep
- Cost Price

# Procedure 🏢 
- Firstly, I started with Data Cleaning i.e checking for missing values, and performed data transformation i.e creating new values to prepare for analysis like *Revenue, COGS(Cost of Goods Sold), Profit* and *Month*
- Began analysis by creating KPIs for Revenue, COGS, Profit and Customer valuables.
- Then, created Pivot tables to summarize the data in order to find key insight and represented these insights on various visualizations.
- Lastly combining every piece to create a Dashboard.

# Finding 📊 
Throughout the analysis, it was discovered that:
- The highest total Revenue was performed by *Peter* of about 434 Million Naira.
- The most profitable product in the entire sales was *Laptop A13* with a profit of about 100 Million Naira.
- In a perion of 5 months; that is from January to May, the month of May has the lowest customer performance of about 0.86% and the month of January had the highest customer performance of about 25.55%.
